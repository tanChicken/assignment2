Global Exchange Rate Analysis

An in-depth look at currency appreciation and depreciation trends from Sep 2023 to Sep 2024.

Bar Chart

This visualization displays the effective exchange rate changes by country from September 2023 to September 2024, indicating whether a country's currency appreciated or depreciated in this period. Countries with positive values show appreciation, while negative values indicate depreciation. The color scale ranges from red (significant depreciation) to green (appreciation), providing a clear overview of currency stability across the globe.

World Map Projections

This visualization shows the trend of the currency exchange rates over time, providing a detailed look at how rates have fluctuated. Each line represents a different currency, allowing for easy comparison of multiple currencies across different dates. Use this chart to understand trends, detect volatility, and identify periods of significant exchange rate changes.
